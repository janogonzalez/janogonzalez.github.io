Still doing a lot of interviews, but finally reaching the end in some of the interview loops. So far I've been through 21 recruiter screenings, the preliminary results are:
- 12 moved to the interview loop
- 5 rejections
- 1 withdrawn because the company is too early stage
- 3 withdrawn because they don't offer visa sponsorship for Spain

Haven't dedicated much time lately to Japanese. I'm only doing reviews in [WaniKani][].

"Graduating" again some albums that have been on my playlist for a few weeks, to give space to new ones.

## Listened to

Albums/EPs:
- Favorite of the week: [Cryptic Shift - Overspace & Supertime][] (Also on [Bandcamp][cryptic-bandcamp]). I've been listening to this album since it was released. A future Technical Thrash Metal classic, it also has some Technical Death Metal Elements that reminded me a bit of Cynic and Atheist.
- [aespa - Lemonade][]. Not that good but at least more consistent than their [previous full album][]. The title track is a banger though
- [Concrete Vehicles - Station on the Hill][] (Also on [Bandcamp][concrete-bandcamp]). So good! It's like an alternate reality where the Windmill pub is located in Canada instead of the UK
- [Ed O'Brien - Blue Morpho][] (Also on [Bandcamp][ed-bandcamp]). Great release, very psychedelic and to make things even better, one day I was walking in a park listening to this album and I heard the birds singing through my headphones
- [I-Rohm - Home][]. Another good EP from this independent K-Pop artist
- [Macha y El Bloque Depresivo - Bronceado de Cantina][]. Great music but I have the moral obligation to say their [first album][] is even better. For the non Chilean readers: "Bronceado de Cantina" is "Bar Tan", the red color in your face after drinking too much
- [Miasme - Keep Them at a Distance][] (Also on [Bandcamp][miasme-bandcamp]). Good Post Metal with many Black Metal elements
- [tripleS - Love&Pop Pt.1][]. Nice K-Pop EP
- [tsubi club - trinket][] (Also on [Bandcamp][tsubi-bandcamp]). Tons of cool sample mangling
