Finishing some recruitment processes. Trying to sync all the offers is still challenging, had to decline a few offers while I'm going through team matching in another process.

Shitposting on LinkedIn as a [backpressure][] mechanism to slow down job offers. Now the post that says I'm looking for a job is buried in the scroll down.

Not much progress learning Japanese in the last 6 weeks. Job hunting has been time consuming.

Got a bunch of vinyl records in the last few weeks:

- [A Forest of Stars - Stack Overflow in Corpse Pile Interface][]. One of my favorite Avant-Garde Metal releases this year
- [Bekor Qilish - Consecrated Abysses of Dread][]. As above
- [Coroner - Mental Vortex][]. Absolute Technical Thrash Metal classic
- [Cryptic Shift - Overspace & Supertime][]. A future classic, mark my words
- [Fleshvessel - Obstinacy: Sisyphean Dreams Unfolded][]. A favorite from 2025 that I discovered this year
- [Sarcófago - I.N.R.I.][]. A classic from Brazil. One of the most influential early Black Metal releases
- [Talking Heads - Remain in Light][]. A must for any record collection
- [Vektor - Terminal Redux][]. Another Technical Thrash Metal classic
- [Vulcano - Bloody Vengeance][]. Another Brazilian classic

## Listened to

Albums/EPs:
- Favorite of the week: [Bekor Qilish - Consecrated Abysses of Dread][] (Also on [Bandcamp][bekor-bandcamp]). One of my favorite releases this year. I can't believe this is a solo project
- [Haute & Freddy - Big Disgrace][]. The best Synthpop release of 2026. Will probably grab a physical copy
- [Rhododendron - Ascent Effort][] (Also on [Bandcamp][rhododendron-bandcamp]). Great Spotify recommendation
- [Toru - Velours Dévorant][] (Also on [Bandcamp][toru-bandcamp]). Another great Spotify recommendation. Chaos in control

Recently released singles:
- Favorite of the week:[Polyphia - CAN YOU FEEL IT][]. Nice mix of Gen-Z Math Rock and EDM. The [alternate music video][polyphia-alternate] is probably better
- [i-dle - Crow][]. Pretty different to most K-Pop releases nowadays
- [POPPY - Dying To Forget][]. Great screaming
- [RIIZE - Do your dance][]. The instrumental slaps
- [unpeople - friends][]. I need to know more about this band!
- [XLOV - SERVE][]. They actually serve
- [Yves - Break it (feat.Lexie Liu)][]. Breaking my own rule of never mentioning new singles from a release [I already talked about][]

Other:
- [A City Pop version of dodree - LILAC][]
- [At Vance's metal cover of ABBA - The Winner Takes It All][]. On paper sounds like a gimmick but it's pretty good
