# Superbooth

Had a great time at [SUPERBOOTH26][] this week. You can try new electronic
instruments, talk to their makers and listen to many artists playing.

Some highlights include:

- Attending the [pre-show at Ohm][]. [Baseck][] X [KODEK][] was amazing
- Watching again Baseck and KODEK performing at the [Erica Synths][] booth
- The [Gudrun Gut][] DJ set
- The [Lady Starlight x Rødhåd][] live set
- The [Tunegirl x Colin Benders][] live set
- Listening to Bob Coover, co-founder of [Groove Synthesis][] giving an
  impromptu masterclass on [Wavetable synthesis][]. I hope to reach one day that
  level of expertise, passion for your craft and ability to explain technical concepts
- Playing the [Frap Tools Magnolia][]
- Playing the [Miltone 4exp][], a clone of the legendary [Oberheim Four Voice][], and talking to the creator
- Testing the [Chase Bliss Big Time][] delay pedal
