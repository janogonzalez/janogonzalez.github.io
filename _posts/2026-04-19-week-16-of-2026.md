Somehow I'm still not fully recovered from sickness. Still taking things slower than usual.

Started looking for a job in [Málaga, Spain][málaga]. I hope to find a nice gig 🤞.

My family gave me [Magdalena Bay - Imaginal Disk][imaginal] on vinyl. A modern classic.

Went to the cinema for the screening of the April 18 [BTS concert in Japan][bts]. Some songs from the second part of the latest album that originally [didn't convince me][convince] improve in the context of a live concert.

## Listened to

Albums/EPs:
- Favorite of the week: [Yves - NAIL][yves]. The 4th EP from Yves where she continues to move into a more alternative electronic sound. Only 5 songs, I hope the next comeback is a full album

Recently released singles:
- Favorite of the week: [Yves - NAIL (feat.Lolo Zouaï)][nail]. The title track from her new EP
- [BE BOYS - SLAM DUNK][slam]. Some nice beat changes, like the part with a tempo slowdown
- [cosmosy - Silence ~ body & soul ~][cosmosy]
- [HITGS - Cherry Blossom][hitgs]
- [ifeye - Hazy (Daisy)][ifeye]. I like most K-Pop songs with a D&B beat
- [KEYVITUP - KEYVITUP][keyvitup]. That classic Pop boygroup sound
- [My New Band Believe - Pearls][pearls]. I need to listen to their album
- [PHANTOM - Summoned to Kill][phantom]. Old-school sounding Metal

Other:
- [Castle Rat - Full Performance (Live on KEXP)][castle]. Epic Doom Metal
- [DJ Stingray 313 \| Electro & Techno DJ Set \| SECTION. \| April 2026][stingray]
- [GOTH-TRAD \| Boiler Room: Osaka][goth-trad]. Dubstep's not dead
- [Haute & Freddy: Live From KCRW HQ (Full Performance)][haute]. Great presentation from the Synthpop duo
- [Tijuana Panthers - Creature - Live at Coachella 2026][tijuana]. I love this song. Haven't listened to Tijuana Panthers in ages

## Other Media

Playing:
- [Might & Magic: Clash of Heroes][clash]. Starting the second campaign. Didn't play much this week
