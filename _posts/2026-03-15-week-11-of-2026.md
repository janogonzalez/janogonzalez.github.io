Pausing the [Practical Machine Learning: Foundations to Neural Networks Specialization][pml] to find material with more depth than the [first course][course] on Probability. In the end, when learning for fun rather than practical purpose you can take as many detours as you want.

Finally started level 7 in [WaniKani][wanikani]. Also started with the absolute beginner videos in [Comprehensible Japanese][cijapanese].

This month is my birthday so I got some of my favorite records on vinyl:

- [Black Country, New Road - Forever Howlong][black]
- [Deafheaven - Lonely People with Power][deafheaven]. I finally have physical copies of my [top 3 albums of 2025][2025]
- [The Mars Volta - De-Loused in the Comatorium][mars]
- [Megadeth - Rust in Peace][megadeth]

Tried to clear my album backlog. It's great to have enough free time to listen to a lot of music. This week was mostly Shoegaze, Metal and K-Pop.

## Listened to

Albums/EPs:

- Favorite of the week: [Gorrch - Stillamentum][gorrch] (Also on [Bandcamp][gorrch-bandcamp]). A random Spotify recommendation has potential to become one of my top metal albums of 2026
- [BLACKPINK - Deadline][deadline]. Another K-Pop album where I only liked the singles 😭
- [Blackwater Holylight - Not Here Not Gone][blackwater] (Also on [Bandcamp][blackwater-bandcamp]). Great Shoegaze album, it gets pretty heavy in some parts. Recently learned that this sub-genre is called [Doomgaze][doomgaze]
- [Chip Wickham - Love & Life][love] (Also on [Bandcamp][love-bandcamp]). A friend is a huge fan of [Gondwana Records][gondwana] so I'm checking some of their releases semi-randomly. This artist caught my eye (or ear?)
- [Flying Lotus - Big Mama][flying] (Also on [Bandcamp][flying-bandcamp]). Short and interesting electronic music EP
- [Martröð - Draumsýnir eldsins][martrod] (Also on [Bandcamp][martrod-bandcamp]). One the best Black Metal albums from last year. The drums sound is a little bit modern for my taste but still great
- [Nothing - A short history of decay][nothing] (Also on [Bandcamp][nothing-bandcamp]). Some of the songs did nothing for me (see what I did there) but others were bangers
- [Petrichor - Seduced by Conquering Darkness][petrichor] (Also on [Bandcamp][petrichor-bandcamp]). Atmospheric Black Metal, sometimes the melodies sound NWOBHM-esque
- [Softcult - When a Flower Doesn't Grow][softcult] (Also on [Bandcamp][softcult-bandcamp]). Good album, but I expected a little bit more considering their latest EP was one of my [favorites of 2024][2024]. That being said, I liked it more in consecutive listening sessions
- [YENA - Love Catcher][yena]. I expected more from a 5 song EP. The first 2 songs are fun and the last song is a solid ballad
