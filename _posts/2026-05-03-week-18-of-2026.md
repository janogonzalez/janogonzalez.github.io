## About the "Listened to" section

Writing about the weeknotes in the weeknotes. Let's get meta!

The "Albums/EPs" sub-section of "Listened to" only shows music that I've
listened to with a good degree of attention. For example, I've listened to
[Cryptic Shift - Overspace & Supertime][] multiple times but only walking
outside, that's why I haven't written about it yet.

For albums like [Neurosis - An Undying Love for a Burning World][] I need to sit
down and listen to them with the studio monitors before considering them
"listened to".

Finally, in this section I omit albums that are part of my "permanent
collection".

Lately, I've been thinking about making the section more useful. Maybe only
mentioning favorites and adding extra commentary. At the moment the section is
a bunch of links to music ranging from decent to great without much description.

If you actually check the music I post about, let me know what you think!
