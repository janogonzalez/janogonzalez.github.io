## Listened to

Albums/EPs:
- Favorite of the week: [A Forest of Stars - Stack Overflow in Corpse Pile Interface][] (Also on [Bandcamp][a-forest-bandcamp]). Avant-Garde Metal, maybe it can be called Post Black Metal? Great album with the right amount of weirdness. Kind of an arbitrary decision because all albums were great this week
- [Knats - A Great Day in Newcastle][] (Also on [Bandcamp][knats-bandcamp]). I was curious about this album because it's produced by [Geordie Greep][]. Probably one of the best Jazz albums of 2026 or at least one of my favorites
- [Ram-Zet - Sapien][] (Also on [Bandcamp][ram-zet-bandcamp]). Great Avant-Garde Metal, it reminds me of [Igorrr][] a bit

Recently released singles:
- Favorite of the week: [A Perfect Circle - Starless][]. The songs in [Mer de Noms][] are still unbeatable for me but this is a pretty good single
- [aespa - LEMONADE][]. Probably their best single since [Whiplash][]
- [iiso - Best Friend][]. Produced by [Mura Masa][], maybe she's entering the big leagues?
- [IVE - LUCID DREAM][]
- [Kaitlyn Aurelia Smith - RUIN][]. Kaitlyn Aurelia Smith making D&B wasn't on my 2026 bingo card!
- [NMIXX, Anderson .Paak - Caution][]. NMIXX is currently my favorite K-Pop group but this collab didn't do it for me.
- [Milena - Telescope][]. Old-school ballad sound
- [The Ocean - Light Pollution][]. A slow burner
