Finally received the vinyl version of [Candelabro - Deseo, carne y voluntad][candelabro], my [second favorite album of 2025][favorite]. Sadly, there was some damage during transportation to the corners of the cover.

This week I've been a little sick, slowing down my progress in the first course of the [Practical Machine Learning: Foundations to Neural Networks Specialization][pml].

## Listened to

Albums/EPs:
- [Bruno Pernadas - unlikely, maybe][bruno] (Also on [Bandcamp][bruno-bandcamp]). Great Jazz Fusion from Portugal. Found in the [Rate Your Music charts][charts]
- [Femtanyl - Man Bites Dog][femtanyl] (Also on [Bandcamp][femtanyl-bandcamp]). I had great expectations and I wasn't disappointed
- [FKA twigs - Eusexua][eusexua]. One of the best electronic Pop albums from last year
- [FKA twigs - Eusexua (Reissue)][reissue]. A version of the album above with some song changes
- [FKA twigs - Eusexua Afterglow][afterglow]. A follow-up released the same year. For some it's even better than Eusexua. I'm still undecided
