Attended the [Yves concert last Sunday night][yves]. Only 2 days after the release of her [new EP][nail]!

Exchanged the [Moog Matriarch][matriarch] for an [Oberheim OB-6][ob-6]. I want only one synth with keys and for my setup that role is better fulfilled by a polysynth. The last change I want to make for this year is having a drum machine again.

## Listened to

Albums/EPs:
- Favorite of the week: [Como Asesinar a Felipes - Umbral][umbral]. Another solid release from one of the best contemporary Chilean bands. All songs but the last feature a collaboration with another artist
- [cosmosy - ~ Of the World ~][world]. A group with Japanese members from a Korean company. Nice Pop EP
- [FM Skyline - Magical Thinking][magical] (Also on [Bandcamp][magical-bandcamp]). Fun YouTube recommendation. An album made almost entirely with a [Korg M1][m1]
- [NECTA - GIRLHOOD][girlhood]. First full album from this Electronic Pop artist
- [Los Retros - Odisea][odisea] (Also on [Bandcamp][odisea-bandcamp]). Nostalgic City Pop and Yacht Rock sounds
- [Los Tres - XCLNT][xclnt]. First album with the original formation since 1999. One of the weakest releases from one of my favorite bands. At least my expectations were not too high

Recently released singles:
- Favorite of the week: [Milk - In Remote][remote]. It reminds me of Faith No More
- [Boards of Canada - Tape 05][tape]. They are back!
- [Chernaya Rechka - Loveland][loveland]. Depressive Russian Post-Punk
- [Frugit - I Knead Dem Biscuits][knead]. Super danceable and made me learn the word "knead"
- [LE SSERAFIM - CELEBRATION][celebration]
- [Margaritas Podridas - Sierra][sierra]
- [MilMoon - True Mind][true]

Other:
- [비비 (BIBI) - Bumpa \| UOB Afterhours][bumpa]
