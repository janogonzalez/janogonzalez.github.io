Weekly reminder that [I'm looking for a job in Spain](/available-for-work).

This week was packed with recruiter screenings and a couple of tech interviews.

Just like [last week][], got another recruiter screening that lasted less than
5 minutes because they only offered visa sponsorship for the UK and not Spain 😭.

Got some new vinyl records:
- [The Last Dinner Party - Prelude to Ecstasy][]. One of my [favorite albums of 2024][]
- [The Messthetics and James Brandon Lewis - Deface The Currency][]. A 2026 favorite
- [My New Band Believe - My New Band Believe][]. Another 2026 favorite
- [Swans - Birthing][]. I can't believe the [Saturn][] from Alexanderplatz had this triple (!) LP + DVD on sale for 29 EUR

"Graduating" many of the albums I've been listening to the last few weeks to give space to new ones.
Maybe in some point of the year I'll explore classics instead of catching up with new music.

I've been listening to a lot of Electronic Pop albums, will add more Metal to the mix again.

## Listened to

Albums/EPs:
- Favorite of the week: [My New Band Believe - My New Band Believe][] (Also on [Bandcamp][my-new-bandcamp]). One of my favorites of the year. I want to try whatever people are drinking at the [Windmill Pub][], maybe my music will dramatically improve
- [Coffret de Bijoux - Lose Myself In You][] (Also on [Bandcamp][coffret-bandcamp]). Two 15 minutes Blackgaze bangers. I have to admit the synth sections could have been better, but still a great album
- [jody积融 - Is It Gonna Happen Again？][] (Also on [Bandcamp][jody-bandcamp]). Great Electronic Pop recommendation from Spotify
- [Juicelover - .mp3][]. Not so well-known Korean artist with great songs
- [NECTA - GIRLHOOD][]. Full of Electronic Pop bangers
- [The Messthetics and James Brandon Lewis - Deface The Currency][] (Also on [Bandcamp][the-messthetics-bandcamp]). Jazz with a lot of Rock, it will make it to my favorites of the year
- [underscores - U][] (Also on [Bandcamp][underscores-bandcamp]). I was expecting something noisier, so the album initially didn't do it for me. It got better every time I re-listened to it!
- [YUNA - Ice Cream][]. Four solid Pop songs without filler. _Why sing many songs when few do trick_

Recently released singles:
- Favorite of the week: [Q_ARE - FÚ][]. Chilean Pop aka [Chispop][]. Banger with Jersey Club influences
- [ADÉLA - KGB][]
- [The Avalanches - Together ft. Nikki Nair, Jessy Lanza, Prentiss][]
- [BIBI - BUMPA][]
- [Ed O'Brien - Sweet Spot][]. I need to listen to the full album
- [Haken - In a fever dream][]
- [JPEGMAFIA - $ (Money)][]
- [Liminal - The Crop][]. Proggy and psychedelic
- [Muse - Hexagons][]. Muse remembered how to write Muse songs
- [ผ้าอ้อม99999 - TOKYO TECHNOLOGIA!][]. Brazilian Funk but from Japan
- [Xiu Xiu - In Heaven][]
- [ZEROBASEONE - TOP 5][]. Classic boygroup sound
