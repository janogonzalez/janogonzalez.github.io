These are some of my favorite albums and singles of last year.

The selection criteria is similar to [last year][last-year]:

- Released in 2025
- Saved as a favorite
- Listened to at least a few times

This year I made a conscious effort to listen to new music so the list is longer.
I continued listening mostly to K-Pop and Metal but I also tried to discover more music by contemporary Chilean artists.
