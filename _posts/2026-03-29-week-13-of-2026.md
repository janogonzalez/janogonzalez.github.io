Traveled to Frankfurt for a couple of days to attend the [NMIXX concert][concert]. The concert was great but I didn't have time to discover the city.

Had to walk 2 km after the concert. The train wasn't passing in the nearest train station, the bus neither. I joined a bunch of other people and walked to another train station.

Ate roll cake and mochi from [IIMORI][iimori], a French-Japanese Pâtisserie. Tasty but a little expensive.

Got some Ghibli merchandising from [Atomax][atomax] for my family.

Went to the NMIXX pop-up store near the airport to get an overpriced cap, collaborating to [JYPapi][jyp]'s pension fund. The cap wasn't available but I had already spent 30 minutes going there, so I ordered one home. Yay [sunk cost fallacy][sunk]!

Sold the [Serge Mantra II panel][mantra]. It's a great synth but I don't have enough space on my desk (or brain) for it.
