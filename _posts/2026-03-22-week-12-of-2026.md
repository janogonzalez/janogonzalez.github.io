## Listened to

Albums/EPs:

- Favorite of the week: [Antropoceno - No Ritmo da Terra][antropoceno] (Also on [Bandcamp][antropoceno-bandcamp]). Another candidate for favorite album of the year. I need an expert to tell the exact genre of this album, it mixes Brazilian music with electronic and even Post Metal elements
- [Antropoceno - Naturaleza Morta][antropoceno2] (Also on [Bandcamp][antropoceno2-bandcamp]). It was in my list of [2025 albums to revisit][2025]. A great mix of Shoegaze and Brazilian music. Still figuring out whether I like this more than the new album or not
- [BTS - Arirang][bts]. An OK album, my expectations were too high. I liked the first half but a lot of the second half sounded like generic pop to me
- [dreamtheater2 - Where God Ends and Mankind Begins][dreamtheater2]. One of the best band names ever
- [Gong - Bright Spirit][gong] (Also on [Bandcamp][gong-bandcamp]). Good prog. I wasn't familiar with their work and they have being releasing music since the '70s
- [Trauma Ray - Carnival][carnival] (Also on [Bandcamp][carnival-bandcamp]). I expected more after listening to their single Hannibal some weeks ago
