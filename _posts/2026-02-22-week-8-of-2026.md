Another quiet week, the only notable thing was attending the [Geordie Greep concert last Friday night][geordie].

During the last 2 weeks I didn't have any projects. It turns out that without a plan for the day it's really easy to waste time in random stuff. I need some structure to function properly.

I'm finally in level 6 of [WaniKani][wanikani]. This week I had difficulties memorizing the new Kanji, too much information in my memory?


## Media

Playing:
- [The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild][botw]

Watching:
- [Jeffrey Epstein: Filthy Rich][filthy]

Listened to:
